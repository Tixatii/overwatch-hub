Join the Community

Overwatch has a large and passionate community with a deeply established esports scene. While the pinnacle of competitive play features professional athletes competing in the Overwatch Champion Series (OWCS) for cash prizes, and representing their home countries in the international Overwatch World Cup (OWWC), the competitive scene is highly inclusive and extends beyond just the top tier.

Competitive Play at Every Level

Players of all skill levels have the opportunity to engage in structured competitive play. The amateur scene is incredibly active, ranging from friendly community tournaments to more organised mid-tier leagues such as FaceIt. There is also a university scene for competitive Overwatch, for which Bangor University has a team (which the author of this site is a member of).
